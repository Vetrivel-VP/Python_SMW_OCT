Looping Statements:
    Helps you to repeat a logic for sevaral times.

Two Categories:
    Entry Check Looping
    Exit Check Looping

Entry Check:
    It executes the Statements  only if your condition is satisfied.

Two Types:
    for loop
    while loop

    Stages:
        1 Initialization
        2 Condition
        3 Statements
        4 Increment or Decrement
    Increment:
        Increase the variable count by 1
    Decrement
        Decrease the variable count by 1

        If you failed to increment or Decrement then it become an infinite loop.
